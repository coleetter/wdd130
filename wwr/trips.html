Trips

Red River George

We think this river was named after my neighbor George. He's kinda an odd fella, always mows his lawn without a shirt. But this one aint for the faint of heart. We like to call this one "Restless Sleep" because you get tossed and turned the entire way down. There's also a waterfall at the end that we try to pull you out of before it's too late but we're not always successful.

Grand Canon River

Catch El Pouder is a great intermediate river that's fun for all ages. We have lost quite a few people on this river but they usually turn up eventually. We think the river lets out in New Mexico but the locals were really hard to understand so we couldn't quite figure out where we were. Real nice people though.

Grand Canon River

This is our newest available river. No one has gone down it yet so we're still waiting for someone to buy passes for it so they can tell us more information about it. We just gave it a 5 star review so people will be more likely to try it. It has to be pretty good though. It has "grand" in the name. So try it out and tell us what you think!
